FAQ

Do you collect sensitive data?

We keep it simple. You can use a first name or a nickname. We only need enough info to send your PDF and generate charts.

How do promo codes work?

You’ll receive a one‑time token. Click “Enter promo code,” type your token, and the form will open with your token attached. On submit, the token is validated before the report is generated.

Can professionals use this?

Yes. During the pilot, clinics can request multi‑use tokens to invite families to try it for free.
